## Catalog scope

The built-in Grade 3 catalog contains:

- 37 grade-specific Mathematics content entries
- 8 Standards for Mathematical Practice
- 92 English Language Arts/Literacy catalog entries, including parent standards and lettered substandards
- 2 ELA grade-table placeholders retained for completeness but marked non-selectable: RL.3.8 and W.3.9

## Content not bundled

Level Learning PDFs, extracted Level Learning standards/objectives/rubrics, learner data, schedules, calendars, lesson content, tasks, and other user-supplied curriculum materials are not included in the public source or deployment package. They may be installed locally by the user where permitted.
